ABOUT ME


In my professional journey within equestrian-oriented roles, I've cultivated adaptability, passion, and creativity. Within this realm, I discovered my aptitude for contributing to the development and launch of online auction platforms, as evidenced by my successful endeavors with Equiauction and the US-branded bloodstockauction.com.

After transitioning from the equestrian space, I embarked on a three-year tenure as a finance broker in the dealership industry. This experience provided me with valuable insights into the world of brokering and the intricacies of the financial landscape. Motivated by my unwavering hunger for innovation and collaboration, I sought out an executive role that allows me to flourish by embracing both of these elements. My innate drive to push boundaries and think outside the box finds a perfect match in this position.
